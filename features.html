Feature Details

Our features are simple but powerful to provide easy to use with great results for business

  • Dashboard
  • Operation
  • Setting

With Mineboard, the big data can be processed in real-time quickly by the system, so that, generate interactive diagrams that inform the performance of your company complete, and can be sorted by range, by shift, by month, or by year dynamically. It is a great dashboard, we can analyze data quickly as needed. Widget contained in the Mineboard Dashboard:

  1. Cost Speedometer, cost parameter becomes the main parameter to assess the performance of the mining company. And you can track with Mineboard!
  2. Operation Widget, the complete module consists of Drill, Blast, Load, Haul, Crush, Sales. Don’t worry, the software is designed modularly, So, it is very flexible with mining activities in your company.
  3. Decision Tree, It helps you to adjust decision making when mining problems are occurred, and will provide recommendations from analysis of problems performed by the system automatically.
  4. Optimized Bar Chart, it can perform the processing of historical data displayed in diagrams and make it easy to analyze further.

Miners in the field need easy input data interaction and don’t make eyes easily tired. Mineboard display is made with a comfortable design to perform data input to the system with soft colors and a proportional pallete combination. The form contained on the Mineboard consists of Drill, Blast, Load, Haul, Crush. Each mining activity has its own form in accordance, so functional can work well in your company. Each Point of View (PoV) has the right to fill the form with constraints in accordance with the authority. It was created to provide functional software that can increase your company's productivity. The PoV form filling involved are:

  1. Operator, inputting data field conditions in accordance with the plan that has been done
  2. Warehouse / Material Management, inputting data related to mining equipments
  3. Supervisor / Engineer, inputting data as a field control.
  4. Workshop, inputting data from the evaluation of mining operations.
  5. Dispatch, integration with dispatch so that direct data can be pushed to the server.

Mineboard is made flexible to set up in accordance with mining activities in your company and make it easier for you to analyze the company's performance with the features available. You can adjust according to your company's conditions, including activities, equipment, units, materials, company profile, crew, and other needs in mining operations. Do not worry, the Mineboard database has saved the specifications of mining equipment and models that can simulate mining performance with various conditions so that your mining performance has a standard to improve productivity. Mineboard can also be used to create reporting by exporting data from Mineboard into reports that corresponding to the standard format of Indonesia government. Your work will be much faster and scalable according to your plan.
